CAT BREEDS

Cat Image Cat Breed Description
Scottish Fold These cats are considered rare. Their rich appearance make them expensive, including their kitty habits and good personality.
Siamese Just like the other cats, Siamese cats are good companions and are very loyal. These cats also tend to be sweet and are one of the best family pets among the breeds.
Maine Coon The Maine Coon breed is one of the largest cats among the breeds. They come along with families and children because a lot of people enjoy cuddling with their furriness.
Ragdoll These cats are the calm and peaceful ones. They are friendly, so they are sociable with other animals and humans. It is said that Ragdoll cats nearly have a dog-like energy to their owners. Therefore, these cats are perfect for a companion.
Sphynx Some owners face problems with their Sphynx cats such as room temperature, due to them being furless. Although these cats are very sweet and are good companions if you choose to spend time to play with them.
Burmese This breed is one of the best family pets. These cats are sweet, loyal and very affectionate with their owners. These cats are good companions for little children.
Abyssinian The Abyssinian cats are one of the most popular breeds. They are great home cats. Just like other cats, they are affectionate. These cats love playing with their owners.
Persian These are calm and quiet cats. They tend to be sweet at times but they aren't attention seekers. These cats rather sit on something soft and enjoy their personal space in peace.
British Shorthair British Shorthair cats are very loyal companions. But sadly, these cats don't enjoy being cuddled or petted. You may treat them as normal human beings by greeting them or sitting down beside them, or conversing with them.
DeroN Rex Devon Rex cats are very intelligent and friendly. They easily adapt to their environment and owners. They like to be around pets and other people while they enjoy the attention of being petted.
